BIOGRAPHY


Puneeth Rajkumar is an Indian film actor, playback singer, and television presenter.
He was born on March 17, 1975, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India, to the legendary Kannada actor Rajkumar.
Puneeth Rajkumar made his acting debut as a child artist in the 1982 film "Parashuram."
He then went on to appear in several other films as a child artist
Puneeth Rajkumar is widely regarded as one of the top actors in Kannada cinema and has won several awards.
He is also a recipient of the National Film Award for Best Child Artist for his performance in the film "Bhakta Kumbara."
